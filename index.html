Behind the Bar

Kline's Dairy Bar has been around since 1943. Our old fashion way of making ice cream has kept our customers happy for 80 years! That is what makes our soft serve so rich and creamy is the specific process we have in making our fresh ice cream. Each morning the Downtown Harrisoburg shop makes ice cream for the day!
